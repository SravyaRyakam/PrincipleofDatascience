Gender: This category specifies the student's gender, with options "male" and "female."

Race/Ethnicity: This attribute identifies the racial or ethnic group to which the student belongs.

Reading Score: This field holds the student's score in the subject of reading.

Writing Score: This field retains the student's score in the subject of writing.
